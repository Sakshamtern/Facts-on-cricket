Cricket

About cricket: Cricket is a bat and ball game played by 2 teams of 11 players each. There is a wicket of 3 stumps on both the side of the pitch. Both the teams do a toss and decides who will bat and who will ball. The baller can ball 6 times in an over. The batters have to hold a bat and hit when the ball is thrown from the baller in the fielding team. The batting team has to make as much runs as possible in order to win. The balling team has to make them out and win when it is there chance to bat. There are many types of cricket like – T 20, IPL (Indian premier league), test match and 1 day match. Cricket is a very famous game. Every 4 years there is a world cup of one day match. The 3 formats of cricket are: a) Test match - Test match is a cricket match of 5 days. Both the teams get 2 innings to play. b) One day match - One day match is a cricket match of 1 day. The teams get 1 inning of 50 overs. There is a world cup in one day match every 4 years. c) T-20 – This is a cricket match or 20 overs. The Indian cricket is very famous in India. India won 1 day world cup twice one in 1983 and 2011. India also won t-20 world cup in 2007 and also won champion’s league. Cricket is one of the richest games in India and around the world.
